English - compre

Types of Compre Qs:

  • Literal - Answers in text
  • Literal - Giving Examples
  • Inferential - From keywords
  • Quotation
  • Global Qs
  • Own words - rephrase keywords

Literal - answers in text :

  • E.g : How do we know Della had monetary issues?

Literal - examples :

  • E.g : From Para_, identify ONE example/detail... -> X Quotation marks (might needa change pronouns)
  • E.g ans : "My mom fed us all early." -> The narrator's mom fed the kids early.

Inferential - from keywords :

  • E.g : What does ... suggest about ... ? -> (personality / feeling / situation)

Quotation :

  • USE quotation marks

Global questions :

  • find main focus of para
  • larger / more obvious answers

Own words - rephrase keywords

  • E.g : What two things were valuable to Della? Answer in your own words. -> find oringinal and rephrase keywords
  • E.g : What... mean by 'daylight nightmare' ? -> rephrase underlined keywords

Notes to take note :

  1. Identify the Qs type
  2. Identify keywords in the Qs -> DO NOT repeat them in your ans
